What to bring:

It’s best to bring your own sewing machine. If you can’t, I have a couple of simple old machines you can use.

You will need to bring your own fabric for most projects. Fabrics made of natural fibre (eg cotton, wool, linen) are easier to make look good and nicer to wear.

Secondhand fabric and repurposed garments can be used for many projects. Look in op shops for good quality zippers and buttons.

If you’re not sure what fabric to choose, get in touch.
