    Side Seam is a sewing studio run by Imogen Keen.

    We teach sewing skills at all levels, as well as design, pattern making, repurposing and mending.

    Lessons can be private, or for two or three people. They can be tailored to suit your needs, or you can learn techniques with an in-house project. In-house projects will only run if at least two people are booked in.

    The Side Seam studio is small, casual and inclusive. There are tea and coffee making facilities, and a table in the garden for breaks.

    We are located in Hackett, in Canberra’s inner north.

    One-To-One Lessons

    Private Sewing Tuition

    Work on your own personal project. Book one session or many in advance.

    2 hours duration.

    Price: $60 p/h

    What to bring:

    It’s best to bring your own sewing machine. If you can’t, I have a couple of simple old machines you can use.

    You will need to bring your own fabric for most projects. Fabrics made of natural fibre (eg cotton, wool, linen) are easier to make look good and nicer to wear.

    Secondhand fabric and repurposed garments can be used for many projects. Look in op shops for good quality zippers and buttons.

    If you’re not sure what fabric to choose, get in touch.
